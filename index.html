About Roehampton University

Roehampton is a very special university. We have a proud history stretching back 180 years and are the only London university with a parkland campus. Our four colleges provide a strong sense of community and our size means it’s easy get to know the people who teach you and those you study with.

At Roehampton we pride ourselves on helping all students, regardless of their background, to develop the skills they need for a successful graduate career and fulfilling life. During your time with us you’ll study alongside our exceptional academic staff, who are engaged in world-class research.

Situated just 30 minutes from central London and a 5-minute walk from Richmond Park, our beautiful 54-acre parkland campus provides the best of both worlds.

Programmes

MBA with placement year

The Roehampton MBA with placement year is designed with early career professionals in mind. As a graduate you will receive a dual award Level 7 Diploma in Strategic Management and Leadership by the Chartered Management Institute (CMI). You will have the option to take a placement year with this MBA. You will gain project management skills and develop the strategic acumen required to direct global enterprises. We will support your development through a Dissertation or Consultancy Project.

2 years full-time
£8,250 per annum

MSc Digital Marketing

MSc Digital Marketing will give you the knowledge to develop and implement digital marketing strategies in a socially responsible manner. You will learn how to analyse complex data for informed decision-making, and develop practical skills in web design, content creation, campaign development and data analysis using a range of techniques and digital products. The programme benefits from the Roehampton Business School’s wider marketing research expertise and practitioner links within the marketing profession in London and abroad. This degree is accredited by The Institute of Data and Marketing (IDM).

1 years full-time / 2 years part-time
£8,250 per annum

MSc Computing

MSc Computing Degree is a conversion programme that requires no previous academic experience in the field of computing, making it the ideal course if you want to change career or develop a new set of skills to complement your existing experience and move to a wider range of exciting roles in computing and IT.

The course focuses on core computing skills such as software development, databases and cyber security, helping you to become a competent and fluent computing professional. You will learn key programming skills across languages and platforms, as well as how to provide data solutions and secure systems.

1 years full-time/2 years part-time
£8,250 per annum

MSc Occupational and Business Psychology

You will develop scientific knowledge and practical skills to offer innovative solutions to workplace problems around employee selection, wellbeing, performance, development, diversity and change. You will receive first-hand experience of working within the Occupational and Business Psychology sector. You will learn how to become an independent consultant and apply the consultancy cycle in your practice. This MSc is accredited by the British Psychological Society (BPS).

1 years full-time / 2-4 years part-time
£8,250 per annum

MSc Global Business Management

You will join the Global Leadership Programme, a programme of professional development workshops and company visits designed to help you develop the skills needed to succeed in a fast-paced global business. You will explore the latest theory and gain practical insights into areas of international business and global business operations and strategies. This programme is dual accredited to a CMI qualification.

1 years full-time
£8,250 per annum

MSc Global Financial Management

MSc Global Financial Management will help transform your mindset and the way you understand global finance and the strategic context within which global firms operate and make financial decisions. You will be prepared for managerial roles in international finance, with a focus on financial management and strategy within global organisations. You will study both the principals of global finance as well as contemporary aspects of investment and capital. This programme is dual accredited to a CMI qualification. This course offers the addition of a placement year with placement preparation workshops.

1 years full-time
£8,250 per annum
